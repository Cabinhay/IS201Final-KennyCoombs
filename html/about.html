Personal Bio:

Hello there! My name is Kenny Coombs and I like to do a lot of different things. Growing up I always loved just trying new things and becoming proficient at them before finding something new and wanting to jump right into that. I played soccer for about 8 years, from about 2nd grade to about 10th grade. My club team in Washington won the State Cup two times in a row which was super exciting for my family and I, as well as a few other local cups during the summers.

In high school I decided that performing looked fun, so I tried out for that years school musical "Guys and Dolls" which was a super fun time. A lot of my friends were already doing it so I just decided to join in and actually ended up getting a lead role. I performed in high school playing roles such as Bert from Mary Poppins, Phil Davis from White Christmas, among others.

I graduated high school with honors and was awarded the "Outstanding Citizen Award" from the faculty in the district.
